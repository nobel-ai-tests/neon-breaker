## üéÆ How to Play

- **Objective**: Destroy all the glowing bricks by bouncing the ball off your paddle.
- **Controls**: Move your **mouse** to control the paddle horizontally.
- **Lives**: You start with 3 lives. If the ball falls past your paddle, you lose a life.
- **Winning**: Clear all the bricks on the screen to win!
